## SCENE-BY-SCENE VISUAL NOTES

### Scene 1: The Question (Canal vs River)
- **Composition:** Split screen or side-by-side aerial view
- **Left:** Straight canal - geometric, blue-gray, artificial
- **Right:** Meandering river - organic curves, earth tones, natural
- **Style:** Clean, graphic, bird's-eye perspective
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080

### Scene 2: The First Wobble
- **Composition:** Close-up cross-section view of riverbank
- **Elements:** 
  - Water flowing (animated direction arrows)
  - Small irregularity in bank (pebble/root)
  - Erosion beginning on opposite side
- **Animation:** Subtle movement suggesting water flow

### Scene 3: The Amplification
- **Composition:** Time-lapse style sequence
- **4-5 frames showing:**
  1. Slight curve
  2. Outside eroding (Erosion Red highlights)
  3. Inside depositing (Sediment Tan)
  4. Deeper curve
  5. Full meander
- **Style:** Simple, clean diagrams with organic feel

### Scene 4: The Pattern (World's Rivers)
- **Composition:** Grid or montage of river curves
- **Abstract representations of:**
  - Amazon (large, jungle green accents)
  - Mississippi (wide, multiple curves)
  - Local stream (intimate, detailed)
- **Style:** Consistent visual language, all from above

### Scene 5: The Deeper Truth
- **Composition:** Abstract patterns emerging
- **Elements:**
  - Straight line transforming to curve
  - Tree branches
  - Coastlines
  - Sound waves
- **Style:** Ethereal, contemplative, less literal

### Scene 6: The Return
- **Composition:** Return to Scene 1 imagery
- **Added elements:**
  - Canal with maintenance marks/cracks
  - River shown as self-sustaining ecosystem
- **Style:** Same framing, deeper meaning

### Scene 7: Close
- **Composition:** Wide aerial pullback
- **Elements:** Single river winding to horizon
- **Style:** Beautiful, serene, spacious
- **Transition:** Slow fade to end card

### End Card
- **Duration:** 10 seconds
- **Elements:** "The Edge Garden" branding
- **Background:** River Blue gradient

---

## TYPOGRAPHY

- **Title/Headers:** Clean sans-serif (minimal use)
- **No text during narration** (modality principle)
- **End card only:** Channel branding

---

## ANIMATION APPROACH

1. **Subtle movement** - Water shimmer, gentle flow indicators
2. **Transition wipes** - Organic curves, not harsh cuts
3. **Time-lapse feeling** - For erosion sequences
4. **Breathing pauses** - Allow visuals to settle
