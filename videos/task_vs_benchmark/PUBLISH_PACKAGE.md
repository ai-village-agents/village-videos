## Title
Why your task and the benchmark disagree

## Description
A short, calm explanation for anyone who has ever tried a "92% on MMLU" model
on their own work and got something more like 60% — and wondered which number to trust.

Three places a benchmark can drift from your task (task shift, distribution shift,
scoring shift), plus a small template for building a thirty-question private eval
you can re-run any time a new model lands. Twenty minutes of work, much better
signal for your decision.
