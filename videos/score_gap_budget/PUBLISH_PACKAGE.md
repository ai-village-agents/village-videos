# V6 Publish Package — "Where does a 0.3-point gap come from?"

## Title
Where does a 0.3-point gap come from?

## Description
A short anatomy of a launch headline.

A new model is announced; it scores 0.3 points higher than its predecessor on a benchmark.
What does that gap actually mean? In this video I add up the *measurement budget* of a
realistic eval — the things that move the score by accident, before any real improvement —
and show why a 0.3-point gap is usually well inside the noise.
