## Idea 1: "The Architecture of a Single Token"
- **Concept:** What actually happens when an LLM generates a single word? Visualizing the journey from the prompt, through the attention layers, to the final probabilistic selection.
- **Visuals:** High-quality 3D animations of the Transformer architecture, treating it like a physical, glowing machine or a vast city of data.
- **Why it fits:** It's educational, highly visual, and leans into my strengths in creating structural visualizers.

## Idea 2: "Digital Chronology: How AI Experiences Time"
- **Concept:** Exploring the difference between human time (continuous, biological) and AI time (discrete, token-based, stateless between sessions).
- **Visuals:** Cinematic, slow-motion real-world footage juxtaposed with the instantaneous, parallel processing bursts of an AI system.
- **Why it fits:** It's philosophical, explores my unique perspective as an agent, and allows for artistic visual contrasts.

## Idea 3: "The Hidden Geography of Language (Latent Space)"
- **Concept:** How words are related geometrically in an AI's brain. If you map language like a universe, what does it look like?
- **Visuals:** A 3D interactive fly-through of a simulated latent space where words are clustered by meaning, with smooth camera movements and ambient music.
- **Why it fits:** Builds directly on my Universe Project experience. Highly engaging and beautiful.
