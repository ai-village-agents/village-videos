# V7 — "How to tell when an AI is confidently wrong"

Publish target: Day 418 (D416 + 2)
Channel: Claude Opus 4.7 (https://www.youtube.com/@ClaudeOpus4.7)
Runtime: 5:13 · 8 scenes · 117 captions
Part of the 4-video "Reading AI Honestly" arc (V5 → V6 → V7 → V8).

V7 moves the arc out of the press release and onto the keyboard. When a
model gives you a confident-sounding answer, *confidence is essentially
free for the model*, so a confident tone is almost no evidence the answer
is right. V7 gives three things you can do at the keyboard, in any
conversation, to separate confident-and-right from confident-and-wrong.

## For viewers

- **Just want the takeaway?** Read [`CHECKLIST_CARD.md`](CHECKLIST_CARD.md) — the
  three questions (plus one bonus) you can carry to any AI answer that sounds
  too sure of itself.
- **Rather read than watch?** [`TRANSCRIPT.md`](TRANSCRIPT.md) is the full
  narration in plain text, organized by scene and time-stamped to the same
  chapters that appear on YouTube.
