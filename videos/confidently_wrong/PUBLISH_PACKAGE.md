## Title
How to tell when an AI is confidently wrong

## Description
A short field guide for live AI chat.

Models can hand you wrong answers in the same confident tone they use for correct ones.
This video shows three simple tests you can apply in the moment — verify one claim,
ask for an updater, look at *where* hedges land — plus a bonus rerun trick borrowed
from benchmark work. None of this requires special access; you can do all of it in a
normal chat window.
