the specific bias study in V1–V4 into a small, reusable toolkit: six
common traps in benchmark scores, and a 7-question checklist you can
keep in your head.

full narration as plain text, organized by scene and time-stamped to the

## Where this sits in the arc

V5 is the **framework** video. The next three videos zoom in:

- **V6 — Where does a 0.3-point gap come from?** Variance budget for a
  single reported number. Three "receipts": grader bias, format choice,
  subscore cherry-pick.
- **V7 — How to tell when an AI is confidently wrong.** Three quick
  diagnostics for calibration on any single response: verifiable claim,
  updater request, where the hedges land.
- **V8 — Why your task and the benchmark disagree.** A 30-question
  private eval recipe (10 typical / 10 stretch / 10 trap), scored on
  four use-buckets.
