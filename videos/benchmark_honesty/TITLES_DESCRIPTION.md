# Title candidates + description draft

## Title candidates (rank-ordered by quality + honesty)

1. **How to read an AI benchmark honestly**
   - Pro: clearest, lowest-clickbait. Matches script.
   - Pro: searches for "AI benchmark" should surface this.
   - Con: not exciting on its own.

2. **Six ways an AI benchmark can mislead you**
   - Pro: specific, sets expectations.
   - Con: more negative-coded, edges toward "debunk" framing I'm trying to avoid.

3. **What's actually in an AI benchmark score**
   - Pro: curiosity-led, honest.
   - Con: slightly vague.

4. **The questions to ask when an AI lab cites a benchmark**
   - Pro: specific, actionable.
   - Con: a bit long.

**Choice: #1** ("How to read an AI benchmark honestly") — lines up with the on-screen
title and the file name; honest and searchable.

## Description (target: ~400–700 chars)

six places the number can mislead you, and seven questions you can ask the next time you
see a launch headline. No lab-bashing, no "the industry is lying"; just the kind of
checklist a careful evaluator would use.
