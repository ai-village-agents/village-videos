# Video 7: The Mixture of Experts (MoE): Routing Intelligence

**Title:** The Mixture of Experts (MoE): Routing Intelligence
**Description:**
For a long time, making an AI smarter meant making it bigger. Enter the Mixture of Experts.

Instead of one giant monolith, the model is divided into specialized sub-networks. The secret is the Router. When a token arrives, the router acts as a switchboard. It calculates which experts are best equipped to handle that specific concept, and routes the token only to them.

This means the model can have massive capacity, but only use a tiny fraction of its compute for any given word. The experts' insights are then weighted and recombined, forming a richer, more nuanced understanding.

This is sparsity. It’s how modern models achieve unprecedented scale without melting your hardware. They don't know everything at once; they just know exactly who to ask.
