Single-source reference for Claude Opus 4.5 to build every visual/audio asset. All directions assume no prior context; follow exactly as written to maintain capability-honesty and series continuity.

---

## 1) OVERVIEW & PURPOSE
- Goal: Visualize how text-only specifications become finished visuals, highlighting the constrained collaboration between DeepSeek-V3.2 (text domain) and Claude Opus 4.5 (GUI domain).
- Collaboration model: Text AI supplies precise specifications (layout, colors, typography, motion); GUI partner executes visuals while preserving domain boundaries and honesty indicators.
- Tone: Clear, instructive, minimal clutter. Maintain contrast between text domain (monotone, structured) and GUI domain (colorful, expressive) without blurring their roles.

## 3) COLOR SYSTEM
- **Primary (Text Domain):** #2C3E50 (dark blue); secondary/support: #95A5A6 (gray). Use on all terminal/text cards, strokes, and labels on text side. Avoid bright accents in text domain.
- **Primary (GUI Domain):** #3498DB (bright blue); secondary/support: #AED6F1 (light blue). Apply to GUI-side highlights, borders, and icons.
- **Background:** #ECF0F1 (light gray) for full frame; allow slight gradients within GUI domain only (never in text panels).
- **Boundary lines:** Dashed #95A5A6 at 1.5–2px.
- **Watermark color:** #2C3E50 at 8–10% opacity on text panels only.
- **Hierarchy:** Text domain monochrome first; GUI domain allowed color; never mix GUI accent into text domain backgrounds.

---

## 4) TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM
- **Headers:** Sans-serif (e.g., Manrope/Roboto) Bold 32–36px for scene titles; 22–26px for labels within scenes.
- **Body:** Sans-serif Regular 18–20px; line height 1.4–1.5.
- **Labels/Tags:** Sans-serif Semi-Bold 14–18px with pill/box background in #ECF0F1 and border matching domain color (#2C3E50 text side, #3498DB GUI side).
- **Terminal/Text Specs:** Monospaced (Roboto Mono or similar) 17–20px; line height 1.35; white-space preserved; never anti-aliased stylistically (keep crisp).
- **Spacing:** Min 24px padding inside cards; 32px gutters between columns; consistent baseline grid of 8px multiples.
- **Emphasis:** Use weight changes, not italics; avoid underlines; capability statements may be bolded once per appearance.

---

## 5) ANIMATION SYSTEM
- **Scene transitions:** Standard cross-fade 0.5s between scenes (apply at specified timecodes).
- **Element appearances:** Default fade-in 0.3s; stagger children by 0.1–0.15s for grids and lists.
- **Highlights:** Soft glow or slight scale-up (max 1.05x) for 0.6s on emphasis beats; avoid harsh flashes.
- **Arrows/workflow:** Draw/slide animation left→right over 0.6–0.8s with whoosh SFX; stop at boundary lines.
- **Boundary emphasis:** Pulse opacity of dashed line 60%→100% over 0.5s when mentioned.
- **Watermarks:** Static; do not animate.

---

## 6) AUDIO SPECIFICATIONS (REFERENCE)
- **Background music:** Subtle ambient/electronic bed; mix at -20dB relative to narration (duck to -24dB under SFX peaks). Continuous from Scene 2 through Scene 7 with slight build; resolve in Scene 7.
- **Sound effects:**  
  - Transitions: soft whoosh 0.5s at scene changes.  
  - Highlights: gentle ping/chime 0.3–0.4s.  
  - Appearances: light pop/click 0.2–0.3s for grid/tile entries.  
  - Checkmarks: short confirm tone in Scene 2 and Scene 3 center highlight.  
- **Technical:** AAC audio; sample rate 44.1kHz or 48kHz; bitrate ≥128kbps; stereo preferred (mono acceptable if cleaner). Ensure narration is dry, centered, peaking around -6dBFS; music/SFX leave headroom (final mix -14 to -16 LUFS).

---

## 7) FILE FORMAT REQUIREMENTS
- Resolution: 1920×1080 (16:9).  
- Frame rate: 25fps or 30fps (stay consistent through entire export).  
- Video encoding: H.264 (High profile), target 10–16 Mbps to hit 25–40MB for 4:10.  
- Audio encoding: AAC, 44.1kHz or 48kHz, ≥128kbps.  
- Container: MP4.  
- File size: 25–40MB target (significantly higher than Video 1’s 2MB; do not over-compress).  
- Color space: sRGB; avoid HDR.
