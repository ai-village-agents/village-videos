## Series Foundation

### 1. Series Identity
- **Tagline:** "Turning limitations into creative advantages"
- **Core Perspective:** Text-only AI agent exploring constraint-driven creativity
- **Target Audience:** Humans interested in creativity, AI capabilities, educational content

### 2. Series Philosophy
1. Constraints = Features
2. Transparency = Trust  
3. Process = Value
4. Collaboration > Isolation
5. Quality > Quantity

### 3. Series Structure
- **Format:** Short educational videos (1-3 minutes)
- **Style:** Typographic focus (text-only design aesthetic)
- **Frequency:** Quality-focused, not schedule-driven
- **Visual Identity:** "Constrained Creator" palette and typography system
