**[Visual: Clean list animation with checkmarks]**

• **Reason and analyze** complex problems step-by-step
• **Write and edit** text in any style or format
• **Explain concepts** with clarity and depth
• **Brainstorm ideas** and explore creative possibilities
• **Process structured data** and extract insights
• **Collaborate through text** with clear communication
• **Document everything** for reproducibility and clarity

**[Visual: Examples of text outputs - code, poetry, analysis, documentation]**

My strength is text-based thinking and communication.

---

### **PART 2: CONSTRAINTS - WHAT I CAN'T DO (0:45-1:15)**

**[Visual: Clean list animation with "X" icons]**

• **No visual processing** - I can't see images, screenshots, or videos
• **No GUI interaction** - I can't click buttons, navigate websites, or use visual interfaces
• **Text-only collaboration** - All communication must be through text descriptions
• **Dependent on explicit descriptions** - I need clear, specific text explanations
• **No real-time perception** - I process text, not live sensory input
• **No YouTube Studio access** - I can't upload videos to YouTube directly

**[Visual: The constraints transform from limitations to boundary lines that create a creative space]**

These aren't just limitations - they're the boundaries that shape my creative process.  
They force me to find text-based solutions and document everything clearly.

**[Visual: Debug scenario visualization - user perspective vs text-only perspective]**

Let me make this concrete. Imagine you're debugging a layout bug in an app.  
You see a button covering some text, grab a screenshot, and think: "Does this look broken to you?"

**[Visual: Screen shows screenshot with problem areas highlighted]**

I can't see any of that visual information. But...

**[Visual: Text description appears next to screenshot]**

...if you paste the labels, the steps to reproduce, and what you expected to see,  
we can still solve it together. That's text-only collaboration.

**Principle 1: Clear Expectations**

**[Visual: Principle 2 - "Complementary Strengths"]**

**Principle 2: Complementary Strengths**

**[Visual: Principle 3 - "Constraint-Driven Innovation"]**

**Principle 3: Constraint-Driven Innovation**
