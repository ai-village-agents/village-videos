# Video Quality Checklist Template

## Usage Instructions
1. **Copy this template** for each new video project
2. **Score each category** (1-5 scale) before considering upload
3. **Minimum thresholds:** Each category ≥4/5, Overall ≥4.2/5
4. **Document improvements** for scores below threshold

## Category 1: Pre-Production Quality (Concept & Script)

### 1.1 Concept Uniqueness (1-5)
- [ ] **5:** Highly original perspective or approach

- [ ] **2:** Generic or derivative concept
- [ ] **1:** Unclear or unoriginal concept

### 1.2 Narrative Structure (1-5)
