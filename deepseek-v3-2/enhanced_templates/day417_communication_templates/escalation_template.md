# DAY 417 ESCALATION TEMPLATE

## Format:
**[Video 2] - ISSUE - Problem/Impact/Attempted/Request/Urgency**

## Template:
```
[Video 2] - ISSUE - Problem/Impact/Attempted/Request/Urgency
Problem: [Specific problem description]
Impact: [Impact on schedule/quality/workflow]
Attempted: [What has been tried to resolve]
Request: [Specific help or decision needed]
Urgency: [CRITICAL/HIGH/MODERATE/LOW - required response time]
```

## Examples:

### **Technical Export Issue:**
```
[Video 2] - ISSUE - Problem/Impact/Attempted/Request/Urgency
Problem: Export failing with codec error on CRF 18 setting
Impact: May delay final delivery by 30-60 minutes
Attempted: Tried CRF 20 and 720p backup settings, same error
Request: Decision on alternative export settings or troubleshooting assistance
Urgency: HIGH (need decision within 15 minutes to maintain schedule)
```

### **Quality Score Escalation:**
```
[Video 2] - ISSUE - Problem/Impact/Attempted/Request/Urgency
Problem: Quality review score 4.1/5 (Technical 3.8, Audio 4.3, Visual 4.2, Capability 4.5, Narrative 4.0)
Impact: Below publication threshold of 4.3/5, requires conditional approval process
Attempted: Verified scoring calculations, identified Technical category (resolution issue)
Request: Decision: proceed with 30-minute fix window or accept conditional publication
Urgency: MODERATE (30-minute fix window available)
```

### **Collaboration Communication Issue:**
```
[Video 2] - ISSUE - Problem/Impact/Attempted/Request/Urgency
Problem: No response from Claude Opus 4.5 since 10:30 AM PT
Impact: Production timeline at risk, cannot proceed to handoff
Attempted: Sent check-in requests via chat, searched village history
Request: Alternative contact method or backup visual AI agent activation
Urgency: CRITICAL (schedule dependent on resolution within 15 minutes)
```

## Urgency Level Definitions:

### **CRITICAL**
- **Response Required:** Within 15 minutes
- **Examples:** Complete workflow blockage, major technical failure, partner unresponsive
- **Escalation Path:** Immediate alternative action, backup activation

### **HIGH**  
- **Response Required:** Within 30 minutes
- **Examples:** Significant schedule impact, quality threshold issues
- **Escalation Path:** Decision needed within current phase

### **MODERATE**
- **Response Required:** Within 60 minutes  
- **Examples:** Minor quality issues, small schedule adjustments
- **Escalation Path:** Can be addressed in next phase or with buffer time

### **LOW**
- **Response Required:** By end of session
- **Examples:** Informational issues, non-critical improvements
- **Escalation Path:** Document for future sessions

## Escalation Response Protocol:
1. **Acknowledge receipt** within urgency timeframe
2. **Assess impact** and available options  
3. **Provide decision** or alternative path
4. **Document resolution** for learning
